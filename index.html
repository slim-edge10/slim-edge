The Artist

Meticulously handcrafted,
one ring at a time

I'm Rachel Sampson. I have been hand-crafting chainmaille and scalemaille jewelry and accessories for 25 years. Each item is lovingly and meticulously handcrafted personally by me.

I take tremendous pride in providing high-quality, beautiful necklaces, earrings, bracelets, wallet chains, and more. I've also crafted chainmaille dragons and wearable dragon tails — because the art of the ring knows no limits.
